\section{F-test of equality of variances}
The test statistic is

\begin{equation} 
F = \frac{S_X^2}{S_Y^2}
\end{equation}

has an F-distribution with $n-1$ and $m-1$ degrees of freedom if the null hypothesis of equality of variances is true.
